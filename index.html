pahela baishakh

Pahela Baishakh (Bengali: পহেলা বৈশাখ, romanized: Pahela Boishakh) is the first day of the Bengali calendar which is also the official calendar of Bangladesh. This festival is celebrated on 14 April in Bangladesh and 15 April in the.

nabanna utshob

The festival is celebrated with mela which are called Nabanna Mela. The villagers and locals from both the major religious groups join the with equal participation. The festival gets a lot of support from the creative army of Bengali.

pitha utshob

Pitha (rice cakes) occupies an important place in our culinary culture. For Bengalis it has been a long tradition to make varieties of pitha, particularly in the winter. As the grandmother makes delectable pitha, the children await.
